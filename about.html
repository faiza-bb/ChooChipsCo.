Cookies for a Cause

Sweet treats made with sweetness.

Choo Chips Co. began as a dream shared by friends with a love for baking and a heart for giving. We wanted to create more than just delicious cookies and cute pictures. ChooChips is here to create meaningful impact and carry love, awareness, and hope, especially for the people of Palestine. Since 2025, our story has been about spreading sweetness and making a difference, one lovingly baked cookie at a time.





Our Mission

Bake and sell unforgettable cookies while opening local bakery branches and growing our social platforms. Donate 50% of every sale's profits to local food banks and authentic Palestinian charities.

Our Vision for 2026

To spread love and sweetness through homemade cookies and raise awareness for the people suffering opression, injustice and poverty. Make a meaningful difference, one cookie at a time.
